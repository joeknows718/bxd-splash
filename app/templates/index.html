The Bronx Digital Pipeline

The Bronx Digital Pipeline (BxDP), lead by The Knowledge House in partnership with #YesWeCode and the Student Success Network, is a long term project of the South Bronx Rising Together collective impact initiative.

35% of young adults in the Bronx are disconnected from school and work.

BxDP aims to:

  1. Increase tech literacy and resources to Bronx youth in order to cultivate their interest in tech careers
  2. Strengthen the school to career tech pipeline by connecting the K-12 tech educators in the Bronx like TEALS, Code/Interactive, and Digital Ready schools to workforce development organizations like The Knowledge House, and Per Scholas
  3. Connect young adults to college access resources, internships, networking opportunities and job matching services for entry and persistence in the technology ecosystem.
